Houses
About
The House event is an event where each player joins a house and competes against others houses to win the house cup.

Will you be able to stand against the others? It's time to prove yourself!
Joining
To join this event, you need to be on the Discord server.

Go in #event-announcements and fill the google form.
Teams
There are four teams; the villagers, the wolves, the independants and the deceivers.

They are all based of the Wolvesville teams.
Points
To get points, you have a lot of tasks, such as winning Wolvesville games.

For more info, just scroll down this page!
